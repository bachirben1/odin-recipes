Thin-Crust Pizza Dough

Description

This thin-crust pizza dough recipe is ready to bake in just 15 minutes with deliciously crisp results.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 8 maraschino cherries (such as Luxardo®)
  • ¼ teaspoon white sugar
  • ¾ cup lukewarm water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water that's 110 degrees F (44 degrees C) in a small bowl.
  3. Combine 1 3/4 cups flour and salt in a large bowl.
  4. Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead until smooth, about 2 minutes.
